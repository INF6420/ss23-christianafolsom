Hello There Friend!

I'm Christiana Folsom (She/Her) and I'm so glad your visiting my website!

As a library professional, I am passionate about bringing equal resources to my community. I love collaborating with young people and creating quality programs that meet the needs of my library's children. I work with patrons of all ages, but my current positions focuses on programming for children birth- five years old. In my work as a MLIS graduate student I am learning the skills I need to advance further in Librarianship and improve the services I provide to my community.


On this website you will find tips, tricks and highlights from my 10+ years working and studying as a librarian. I've done everything from storytimes to adult programs and I'm sharing it here with you!
